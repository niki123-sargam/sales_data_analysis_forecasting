## ğŸ“Š Key Insights & Findings
The analysis and dashboard revealed several important business insights:

- **Strong Geographical Concentration**

- **Seasonal Sales Patterns**

  The business demonstrates consistent sales growth over time, highlighting stable demand and effective product-market fit.

- **Time-Based Trends for Forecasting**  
  Quarterly sales trends provide a strong foundation for future sales forecasting and demand planning.
